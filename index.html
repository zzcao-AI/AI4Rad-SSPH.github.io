AI4Rad Lab

Medical imaging AI research at Shanghai Sixth People's Hospital, Shanghai Jiao Tong University School of Medicine.

Team · Google Scholar · GitHub · Email

Research

  • Vision foundation models for detection, segmentation, and quantitative biomarkers in vascular and musculoskeletal imaging.
  • Multimodal large language models integrating imaging, reports, and clinical data for structured reporting and diagnostic reasoning.
  • Medical image generation, denoising, and enhancement to improve image quality and reduce scan time.
  • Clinical AI generalization across sites, scanners, and patient distributions.

Selected Projects

BrainMIND — LLM-based diagnostic impression generation from brain MRI reports. npj Digital Medicine, 2026. Paper · Code

Cerebrovascular & Stroke AI — Aneurysm detection on CTA, automatic ASPECTS scoring, and thrombus characterization in acute stroke. Radiology / European Radiology.
